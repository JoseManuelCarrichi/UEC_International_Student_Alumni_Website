Annual Alumni Homecoming 2024

Date: Saturday, October 28, 2024
Time: 18:00 - 21:00 JST
Location: UEC Campus Auditorium, Chofu, Tokyo

About the Event

You are cordially invited to the Annual Alumni Homecoming, our most anticipated gathering of the year. This is a wonderful opportunity to return to campus, relive your favorite memories, and see how much has changed. The evening will feature a welcome address from the university president, departmental showcases, and plenty of time for networking with fellow alumni, faculty, and current students.

Dinner and drinks will be served. We look forward to celebrating with you!

Schedule

  • 18:00 - 18:30: Welcome & Registration
  • 18:30 - 19:00: Opening Remarks & University Update
  • 19:00 - 20:30: Networking, Dinner & Showcases
  • 20:30 - 21:00: Closing Remarks

Registration

This event is free for all alumni, but registration is required. Please register by October 20th to secure your spot.
