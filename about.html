About the UEC Alumni Network

Our Story & Vision

Founded on the principle of lifelong connection, the UEC International Student Alumni Network was established to serve the global community of The University of Electro-Communications graduates. Our vision is to create a dynamic, supportive, and engaging network that transcends geographical boundaries. We are dedicated to fostering a strong sense of community, facilitating professional and social networking opportunities, and providing a platform for alumni to give back, share their expertise, and stay connected with their alma mater, ensuring our collective legacy continues to thrive and inspire future generations of UEC students.

Why Join Us?

  • ✓ Global Networking: Connect with a diverse community of professionals and innovators across various industries and countries.
  • ✓ Exclusive Resources: Gain access to career resources, workshops, and continuous learning opportunities tailored for UEC alumni.
  • ✓ Stay Informed: Receive the latest news from the university, updates on alumni achievements, and invitations to exclusive events.
  • ✓ A Strong Community: Be part of a supportive network to share experiences, seek advice, and collaborate on new ventures.
